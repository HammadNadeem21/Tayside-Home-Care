Privacy Policy – Tayside Home Care Ltd

At Tayside Home Care Ltd, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent and secure manner. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us regarding our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Address information
  • Enquiries submitted through our website
  • Information related to care services requested
  • Website usage data (cookies and analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information may be used to:

  • Respond to enquiries about our services
  • Arrange consultations or care services
  • Provide relevant information about our care services
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Meet legal or regulatory obligations

We only process your data when we have a lawful basis to do so under UK GDPR.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Healthcare professionals involved in your care
  • Regulatory authorities if required by law
  • Secure IT or website service providers supporting our systems

All partners are required to protect your data.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Request data portability

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please contact:
